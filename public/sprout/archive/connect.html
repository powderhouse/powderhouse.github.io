Our Studios

Our studios are a place for us to prototype our projects and programs. They have a mix of light manufacturing, arts & crafts, wet lab, and electrical engineering equipment. While we've made them public in the past, they are now mainly for use by our organization, but please get in touch if you have a question about them!

Support sprout

If you like what sprout does and would like to make a [tax-deductible] donation to support our future efforts, thank you! We are a registered, 501(c)3 nonprofit and would love to hear from you. Or if you're ready to donate, just enter your information below and we'll email you a receipt and be in touch. If you have any questions or if you've some other type of donation (e.g. equipment, tools, or other materials) to make, drop us a line. We'd love to hear from you, and we are so grateful for the support.
