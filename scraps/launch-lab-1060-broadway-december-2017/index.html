Cost, amenities, availability

  • $40–45 NNN ($3.33–3.75 per square foot per month), but can and will subsidize partners. We can also offer PILOT payments/records for qualifying non-profit entities.
  • 24h access, including world-class prototyping facility, conference rooms, event space
  • On-site, next-door, and on-street parking available, in addition to free Hubway access
  • ~2,000/20,000 square feet is in design for community partners, this is flexible. ~2,500 square feet will be our first cohort's space.
  • This leaves ~15,500 square feet, but this is flexible.
