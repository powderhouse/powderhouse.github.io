Why externalize your thinking?

Animations

Song lyrics

When we externalize things, we are forced to encounter the holes in our thinking, whether we are presenting our thoughts to a computer (in the case of programming) or another person (in the case of a theater production) or our own ears (when reading a piece of writing aloud) or someone with different expertise (when we make a blueprint or storyboard that must be implemented by someone else).
We do projects because we believe, when done right, they set the highest possible standard for what learning something looks like.
