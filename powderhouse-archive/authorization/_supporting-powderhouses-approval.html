Next steps for Powderhouse Studios (and a chance to help)!

Hey there!

We’ve got some exciting news about Powderhouse Studios, and we wanted to tell you about it…and we hope you’ll get involved!

In January 2019, the Somerville Teachers’ Association approved Powderhouse Studios’ union contract, and we’ve finally moved into the last phase of the approval process, including public comment and deliberation leading to a School Committee vote on March 4th.

Until then, the Powderhouse team and Somerville School Committee want to hear from all corners of the Somerville community. So far, there hasn’t been much of a way to formally express support and move the proposal forward, but now is the time your voice can really count.

If you’d like to see Powderhouse Studios happen in Somerville (and/or learn more about its design), there are three things you can do:

  1. Attend a public hearing or another School Committee meeting and sign up to make a public comment in support of the school (scroll down a bit for dates here).

  2. Share your support with School Committee members directly by phone or email or by attending their office hours in person.

  3. Attend a family information session or daytime office hours to learn more about Powderhouse Studios from members of the design team and get support in advocating for the school.

We feel so privileged and excited to have made it this far, with so much support from neighbors, colleagues, and friends.

Please share this message with anyone you think might like to get involved, and reach out with any ideas you have about how we can share this information far and wide.

If you have any questions, please let me know and we’d be happy to chat! — And we’d love to know if you are planning to attend any of the events listed above, so please let me know by replying to this email!

Gratefully,
The Powderhouse Studios team
